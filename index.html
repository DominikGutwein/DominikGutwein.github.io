I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Collaborative Research Center "Higher Structures, Moduli Spaces and Integrability", working in the research group of Prof. Vicente Cortés at the University of Hamburg. I am interested in Differential Geometry and Geometric Analysis, in particular in manifolds with special holonomy, calibrated submanifolds, and gauge theory.
